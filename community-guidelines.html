Sky’s Bridge Foundation

Founding policy

Community guidelines

A protected community requires kindness, boundaries and active moderation.

Respect every form of grief

Members may grieve differently. No one should be pressured to share, forgive, reconcile, publish a memorial or follow a particular timeline.

No harassment or unsolicited contact

Repeated private messages, romantic approaches, financial requests, recruitment and targeted pressure should not be permitted.

No medical certainty

Members may share personal experiences but should not present medical, psychological or legal claims as guaranteed professional advice.

Protect identities

Do not repost another member’s story, images or identifying information outside the platform.

Crisis response

The live platform needs trained moderators, escalation procedures and country-specific emergency resources. It cannot promise emergency monitoring at all times.
