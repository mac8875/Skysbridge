Community guidelines

A gentle, respectful space.

Listen without comparison. Respect each family’s language, beliefs and choices. Do not pressure anyone to share details or publish a memorial. Do not offer diagnoses or present personal experience as medical advice. Protect the privacy of other members and never reproduce their stories outside the community. Harassment, exploitation, fundraising pressure and commercial promotion are not permitted.

Immediate danger

Sky’s Bridge is not an emergency service. Anyone in immediate danger should contact local emergency services or an appropriate crisis service in their country.
