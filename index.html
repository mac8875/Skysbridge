Our mission

No parent should lose their partner after losing a child.

Grief can isolate couples and families even when they love one another deeply. Sky’s Bridge exists to create bridges of understanding, dignity and hope.

Our vision is an international platform where parents can first arrive quietly, enter protected rooms, meet others with similar experiences and—only when they are ready—honor their child on the Wall of Stars.

Safety by design

Trust must come before growth.

The community will be developed with professional review of privacy, safeguarding, moderation and crisis procedures.

✓

Consent controls
Every memorial has clear visibility settings.

✓

Human moderation
Reports and urgent concerns are reviewed by people.

✓

Right to remove
Parents can edit, hide or delete their memorial.

✓

Professional boundaries
Community support does not replace medical care.
