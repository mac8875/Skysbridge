Your child has a name.
Your story has a place.

Sky’s Bridge gives parents and couples a safe place to talk, listen, find support and—only when they feel ready—honour their child on the Wall of Stars.

Why this exists

No child should be reduced to a statistic. No parent should grieve alone.

Members may first observe quietly, enter protected rooms and decide for themselves how much they want to share. The Wall of Stars is an invitation—not an obligation.

Safety by design

Trust must come before growth.

The live platform should launch only after professional review of privacy, safeguarding, moderation and crisis procedures.

✓
Consent controls
Every memorial has explicit visibility settings.
✓
Human moderation
Reports and crisis signals require trained people, not only automation.
✓
Right to remove
Parents can edit, hide or delete their memorial and account.
✓
Professional boundaries
Community support does not replace medical or psychological care.
