Building bridges through grief

A place where every child has a name and every family has a voice.

Sky's Bridge brings grieving parents and families together in a calm, respectful and protected online community.

Members can join private rooms, share their stories at their own pace, find people who understand, and choose whether to honour their child on the Wall of Stars.
