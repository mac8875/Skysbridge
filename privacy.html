Privacy principles

Families remain in control.

This prototype does not yet provide member accounts or memorial publishing. Before launch, Sky’s Bridge will obtain professional legal and safeguarding review.

Core principles

Collect only information required to provide the service. Use explicit consent for memorial publication. Offer private, member-only, private-link and public visibility. Allow members to access, correct, hide and delete their information. Protect sensitive information with appropriate security controls. Never sell grief-related data or place advertising beside memorials.
