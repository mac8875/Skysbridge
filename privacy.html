Founding policy

Privacy principles

This is a project draft, not final legal advice.

Privacy by default

Profiles, memorials and community posts should begin with the most protective practical visibility setting. Public sharing must always require a deliberate choice.

Parent-controlled memorials

Parents should be able to create, preview, edit, hide, export and delete a memorial. Visibility options should include private, private link, members only and public.

Sensitive information

The platform may contain grief, relationship, pregnancy and health-related information. Access controls, secure storage, administrative logging and limited staff access are essential.

Images and family consent

Photos, ultrasound images and details concerning another parent or relative should not be published without an appropriate legal and consent framework.

Before launch

A qualified privacy lawyer should adapt these principles to the chosen legal entity, hosting location and countries served.
